CTF101- A Beginner's Guide to Ethical Hacking through Capture the Flag (CTF)



CTFs are considered as one of the Best Ways to Start your Journey into the Field of Ethical Hacking and provides you with Real Hands-on Experience (Even Cybersecurity Experts recommend it). InfoSec IIT Goa conducted a CTF training session at 4PM on 10th January 2021, to guide students on their path to the world of Cyber Security.
The session covered various topics like Web Exploitation, OSINT, Cryptography, Forensics, Reverse Engineering and Binary Exploitation.
